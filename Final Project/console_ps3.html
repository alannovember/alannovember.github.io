Sony Playstation 3 and 4 ($250 PS3/ $400 PS4) 		Key Features:
  • Powerful processing and impressive graphics - The PS4's advanced architecture allows the games you play to deliver more fully realized worlds and higher player counts than was possible in previous console generations.
  • Innovative controller design - The wireless DualShock 4 controller features a clickable, multitouch capacitive touchpad that can be flicked, tapped or swiped for new gameplay possibilities. USB charge cable included.
  • Plug your headset directly into your controller - The included headset plugs directly into the DualShock 4's 3.5mm jack.
  • Let the world watch you play - The DualShock 4's Share button allows you to stream gameplay footage directly to Ustream or Twitch.tv, or edit videos and share them across social networks including Facebook and Twitter
  • Expand your friend list - The PS4 allows you to create a list of up to 2,000 online friends.
  • Download while you play - Start a game download and begin playing it before the download is complete.
  • Purchase games, movies and more with the PlayStation Store - Sony's online store features a growing library of indie games and free-to-play titles.
  • Remote Play - PS4 games can be played remotely on the PlayStation Vita's 5-inch HD screen over a local wireless network. PlayStation Vita sold separately.
  • Connect with your mobile devices - PlayStation App allows you to view game items, purchase PS4 games and watch your PS4 game footage remotely from a smartphone or tablet.
  • Blu-ray delivers exceptional video quality for your games and movies - Watch Blu-ray and DVD video content. Blu-ray game discs have several times the capacity of DVD-based discs, allowing massive games to fit on a single disc.
